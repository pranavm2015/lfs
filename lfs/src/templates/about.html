Library Faculty System

During the mid semester & end semester examinations in IIT Patna, our library is generally full packed with students so there is hardly any seats left towards the fall of the day. Also students are not able to verify whether professors are in their respective rooms for them to meet so as to clear doubts or for any other purposes.
Our idea is to introduce an online seat booking system for our college library which will be functional throughout the year and an online system to verify whether a person is in his/her respective room or not which would solve this problem completely.

Aim & Objective:

1. Students, before coming to the library would be able to access the seating arrangement of the Library and if they find a suitable seat vacant they could book it upto certain number of hours, say ‘A’ hours. If the student fails to check-in the library within ‘X’ minutes ( for boys ) and ‘Y’ minutes ( for girls ) of the booking time, the seat they had selected would be vacant again for all to see. Each identity card of IIT Patna has a bar code on the front side of it. We would be using Image Processing to read the code and hence confirm the student’s arrival within the given amount of time constraint. Hence the seat would be finally booked for the next ‘A’ hours.
2. The respective rooms for teachers and administrative personnel will have a human detector attached to it to verify of their availability automatically and update it on the site. Students can also book appointments and teachers can approve them online to ease the process.
